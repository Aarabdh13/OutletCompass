Thought for 1s# OutletCompass — Color Palette Reference

## Surface Colors

| Name | Hex | Usage
|-----|-----|-----
| Background | `#0c0b0a` | Page canvas, main background
| Card | `#151418` | Cards, modals, elevated surfaces
| Card Hover | `#1c1a21` | Hovered card state
| Border | `#28252e` | Dividers, card borders


## Accent Colors

| Name | Hex | Usage
|-----|-----|-----
| Primary (Amethyst) | `#9b8ec4` | Buttons, ratings, focus rings, active filters, icons
| Primary Hover | `#b0a5d4` | Hovered buttons and interactive elements
| Secondary (Sage) | `#7a9e7e` | Tag chips, badges, status indicators
| Secondary Hover | `#6b8c6f` | Hovered tags and badges


## Text Colors

| Name | Hex | Usage
|-----|-----|-----
| Primary Text | `#f0ebe3` | Headings, body text, labels
| Muted Text | `#9a9189` | Descriptions, metadata, secondary info
| On Primary | `#0c0b0a` | Text placed on amethyst backgrounds


## Contrast Ratios

| Pairing | Ratio | WCAG
|-----|-----|-----
| Primary Text on Background | 15.2:1 | AAA
| Amethyst on Background | 7.2:1 | AA
| Amethyst on Card | 5.8:1 | AA
| Muted Text on Background | 5.2:1 | AA
| Sage on Card | 4.8:1 | AA (large text)
| Dark Text on Amethyst | 7.2:1 | AAA


## Component Mapping

| Component | Background | Text | Accent
|-----|-----|-----
| Mall Card | Card `#151418` | Ivory `#f0ebe3` | Amethyst stars `#9b8ec4`
| Search Bar | Card `#151418` | Ivory `#f0ebe3` | Amethyst focus ring `#9b8ec4`
| Category Icons | Card `#151418` | Ivory `#f0ebe3` | Amethyst fill `#9b8ec4`
| Filter Pill (active) | Amethyst `#9b8ec4` | Dark `#0c0b0a` | —
| Filter Pill (inactive) | Hover `#1c1a21` | Muted `#9a9189` | —
| Tag Chip | Sage `#7a9e7e` | Ivory `#f0ebe3` | —
| Outlet Card | Card `#151418` | Ivory `#f0ebe3` | Amethyst rating, Sage tags


All pairings meet WCAG AA minimums. Five total colors (warm black, amethyst, sage, ivory, muted) form the complete palette, with card and border tones acting as tonal variations of the background rather than separate additions.
